ABOUT NAMA ICT

The ICT Department of the Nigerian Airspace Management Agency (NAMA) at the Kano branch ensures the seamless operation of air traffic management, navigation, and communication systems. Our team drives innovation and efficiency, supporting NAMA's mission to provide safe and reliable air travel through advanced technological solutions.

System Administration

Managing and maintaining ICT infrastructure for uninterrupted air traffic operations.

Software Development

Creating and deploying software solutions to enhance airspace management efficiency.

Technical Support

Providing 24/7 support to resolve ICT-related issues promptly and effectively.
